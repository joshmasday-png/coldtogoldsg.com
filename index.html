FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need any technical knowledge to use the system?
No. I handle the entire setup, testing, and deployment. You receive a working system and a video walkthrough showing you exactly how everything works. No coding or technical knowledge required.
What WhatsApp plan do I need?
The system uses Twilio — a pay-as-you-go WhatsApp provider. There is no monthly platform fee. You pay only per message sent, which typically costs USD $3–8 per month for 50–100 leads. No expensive subscriptions needed. Works in any country where WhatsApp is active.
How long does setup take?
The full system is live within 48 hours from when you provide your details — your WhatsApp number, agent name, and Calendly link. That's all I need to get started.
What are the ongoing monthly costs?
Make.com free plan, Tally free plan, Airtable free plan, Calendly free plan, and Twilio pay-per-message. Total running cost is typically under ~$15 USD per month for an active solo agent.
Can I sell this system to other agents?
Yes — the system is fully duplicatable. Once you have it running, I can help you clone it for other agents and build a recurring revenue stream. Many agents resell it for $150–200/month per client.
Do you only work with real estate agents?
No. The automation and design services work for any business that generates leads online. Real estate is a specialty, but the same system applies to any business that generates leads worldwide — insurance, mortgage brokers, interior designers, e-commerce, and more.
